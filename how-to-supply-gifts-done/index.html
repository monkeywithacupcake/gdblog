    How to deliver a gift!

    1 minute read

    Suppliers are the best! We count on your creativity and professionalism to make better gifting for everyone.

    Your Dashboard

    When you are logged in as a gifts-done supplier, you can see all of the gift events assigned to you and their status. Most of the time, you will get just over 30 days to make and deliver a gift. The due dates and target shipping countdown are clearly visible at the top of each gift.

    See Gift Details

    When you click on the details, you will see the gift budget, the note that should accompany the gift, the giftee preferences, and the address - all together.

    Finish a Gift

    A finished gift is the best gift, right? Every gift event that is not already finished has “Mark As Finished” button. When you push that button, you are presented with the form

    When you finish a gift, you can double check the details, including the budget, note, and address.

    Ship a Gift

    If the charge is successful after you finish a gift, you will see a “Mark As Shipped” button. Otherwise, you will see a warning that the user could not be charged and you should not ship.

    To identify something as shipped, just press “Mark As Shipped” and add the tracking number. Easy peasy.
