    July Instagram Challenge

    less than 1 minute read

    “Summer is a state of mind”

    Our heart and soul is in our wonderful suppliers, and we want everyone to have a little fun this summer. To that end, we are continuing our instagram challenge for suppliers, and anyone can join the fun. Let’s see what direction your month takes!

    Make the Most of It

    Become part of the party by posting something related to the challenge each day. Be sure to also use and follow the main challenge hashtag #gdempowers - this is how everyone can see your posts and we can repost you!

    • It is so exciting to see how other people use a prompt. Be wild, be wacky, just be yourself.
    • Celebrate other makers and creators
    • Meet new people!

    We’ll Repost You

    We love seeing what our suppliers (and future suppliers) are up to.
