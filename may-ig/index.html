May Instagram Challenge

April 30, 2018

Reading time ~1 minute

“You have brains in your head. You have feet in your shoes. You can steer yourself, any direction you choose.” ― Dr. Seuss

Our heart and soul is in our wonderful suppliers, and we want everyone to have a little fun. To that end, we created an instagram challenge for suppliers, but anyone can join the fun. Let’s see what direction your May takes!

Make the Most of It

Become part of the party by posting something related to the challenge each day. Be sure to also use and follow the main challenge hashtag #gdempowers - this is how everyone can see your posts and we can repost you!

  • It is so exciting to see how other people use a prompt. Be wild, be wacky, just be yourself.
  • Celebrate other makers and creators
  • Meet new people!
