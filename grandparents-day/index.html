    National Grandparents Day

    1 minute read

    Grandparents Day is September 8, 2019

    Love your grandparents? We do. Our grandparents are the best! How awesome is it to find out that there is a day set aside to celebrate them? That’s right, every year on the 1st Sunday after Labor Day we honor the role grandparents play in our lives. I mean, without them, we would not be here - right?

    How can you show your appreciation? Honestly, the best gift of all is your time. Go see them, cook a meal, play some games, listen to their stories, and be in the moment.

    If you can’t be there with them on Sunday, why not send them a little something to show how much they mean to you?

    Here is our list of the top 3 best gifts for National Grandparents Day:

    1. Gift Card Book

    Put together a book gift cards for all the places they love to go. You could use a photo book with the gift card on one side and a picture or story from a time you were with them there on the other.

    2. Future Time Plan

    Send out a hand written invitation to spend time with you in the future. Depending on their interest and your time, the invitation could include tickets to a ball game, movie passes, or even a weekender bag for a get away.

    3. Pictures!

    That’s right - slap pictures of the grandkids or great-grandkids on a mug, a puzzle, or in a cute frame. They love having you close.
